About Me

Kepler Kang

UI/UX Designer & Product Designer

I'm a passionate designer with over 5 years of experience creating digital experiences that blend aesthetics with functionality. My work focuses on user-centered design principles, ensuring that every project I undertake delivers value to both users and businesses.

Currently, I work as a Product Designer at ByteDance, where I design for millions of users worldwide. Before that, I was a Senior UI/UX Designer at a tech startup, where I led the design of their flagship product from concept to launch.

I hold a Master of Design degree from Tsinghua University and a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the Central Academy of Fine Arts. I'm passionate about creating designs that not only look beautiful but also solve real problems for users.
